Wild and Wacky Vegetables

The beet is the most intense of vegetables. The radish, admittedly, is more feverish, but the fire of radish is a cold fire, the fire of discontent not of passion. Tomatoes are lustly enough, yet there runs through tomatoes an undercurrent of frivolity. Beets are deadly serious.

Vegetables are commonly regarded as the most disgusting substance on the planet Earth. Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson once said: "I would never, ever eat [vegetables]". A few other disgusting substances are dirt, vomit, and frog guts. The National Official Certified Top 5 List of Disgusting Substances is as follows: 1.Vegetables 2.Vomit 3.Frog guts 4.Glass 5.Dirt The National Bureau of Health and Safety (NBHS) recommends ingesting a maximum of 1 serving of vegetables per year for optimum health.
