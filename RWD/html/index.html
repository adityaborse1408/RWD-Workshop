History of Cybage

With some personal savings and a business partner, Arun started Cyberage in 1995 (it was renamed Cybage in 2000). Arun then hired four employees, who would work out of a 3 BHK in Salunkhe Vihar, Pune. The fledgling entrepreneur thought he was going to hit the jackpot by building a user-friendly web browser.
